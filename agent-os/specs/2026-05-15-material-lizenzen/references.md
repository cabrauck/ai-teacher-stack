# References for Materiallizenzen und Rechte-Metadaten

Status: Draft

## Official and authoritative starting points

- `https://schulbuchkopie.de/index.php` - overview of scanning and copying in German schools, including the school copying agreement context and current practical rules.
- `https://schulbuchkopie.de/index.php/fragen-und-antworten` - FAQ covering digital works, publisher license conditions, source attribution, and AI-upload boundaries.
- `https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/urhg/__60a.html` - German Copyright Act section on teaching and education uses.
- `https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/urhg/__60b.html` - German Copyright Act section on teaching and instructional media.
- `https://www.unesco.org/en/open-educational-resources/mandate` - UNESCO OER definition and open-license framing.
- `https://creativecommons.org/chooser/` - Creative Commons license chooser and license descriptions, including `CC BY-SA 4.0`.
- `https://bildungsmedien.de/unsere-aktivitaeten/bildungslogin` - context on centralized digital educational media access and license management.

## Research notes

- Use these references as starting points, not as a complete legal assessment.
- Summaries must be written in contributor words and linked to source pages.
- Do not copy publisher terms, textbook content, worksheets, sample pages, or
  other copyrighted teaching material into Agent-OS specs or repository docs.
- Record retrieval dates for sources when research is turned into implementation
  fixtures or stable documentation.
